The short version: CalcHub does not require account creation, does not collect personal information to use the calculators, and does not sell your data. We use standard analytics and serve ads through Google AdSense, both of which may use cookies. Read on for full details.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

CalcHub calculators operate entirely client-side in your browser. Numbers you enter into any calculator are never transmitted to our servers — all computation happens locally. We do not have access to values you type into any calculator field.

We do not require you to create an account, provide an email address, or submit any personal information to use any feature of this website.

Information Collected Automatically

When you visit CalcHub, we or our third-party partners may automatically collect:

  • Log data — IP address, browser type and version, referring URL, pages visited, time and date of visit, time spent on pages
  • Device data — device type, operating system, screen resolution
  • Cookies — small text files stored by your browser (see Section 4)
  • Usage data — which calculators you use, how frequently, and in what sequence

This data is collected in aggregate and is primarily used to understand site performance and improve the user experience. It is not used to personally identify you.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected data to:

  • Operate, maintain, and improve the CalcHub website
  • Understand which calculators are most useful to our users
  • Monitor site performance, uptime, and error rates
  • Detect and prevent abuse, spam, or fraudulent activity
  • Display relevant advertisements (see Section 3)
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not use your data for targeted email marketing, sell it to data brokers, or share it with third parties except as described in this policy.

3. Advertising & Google AdSense

CalcHub uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense uses cookies and web beacons to show ads based on your prior visits to CalcHub and other websites.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to CalcHub and/or other sites on the Internet. You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings or by visiting aboutads.info .

As required by AdSense policy, we inform users that:

  • Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites
  • Google's use of the DoubleClick cookie enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visits to CalcHub and/or other sites on the Internet
  • You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting optout.aboutads.info

For more information on how Google uses data when you use our site, visit: How Google uses information from sites that use our services .

4. Analytics & Cookies

Google Analytics

We may use Google Analytics to collect anonymized data about how visitors interact with our site. Google Analytics uses cookies to distinguish unique users and sessions. This data helps us understand aggregate traffic patterns and improve our calculators.

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on .

Cookies We Use

  • Strictly necessary cookies — Required for site security and basic functionality. Cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics cookies — Help us count visits and understand how users interact with the site. Can be disabled.
  • Advertising cookies — Set by Google AdSense for ad personalisation. Can be opted out via Google Ads Settings.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect site functionality. Most browsers allow you to view, manage, delete, and block cookies via Settings or Preferences.

7. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

European Users (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including:

  • The right to access personal data we hold about you
  • The right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • The right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • The right to restrict or object to processing
  • The right to data portability
  • The right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

Because CalcHub does not collect personally identifiable information to operate, most GDPR requests can be satisfied by clearing your browser's cookies, which removes all data stored locally. For requests related to analytics or advertising data held by Google, please contact Google directly.

California Users (CCPA)

California residents have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information is collected, the right to delete personal information, and the right to opt out of the sale of personal information.

CalcHub does not sell personal information. To exercise your rights, contact us at the address in Section 11.

8. Children's Privacy

CalcHub does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your country). Our site does not target children as a primary audience, though calculators such as the GPA and grade calculators may be used by students of all ages.

If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

9. Security

CalcHub is served entirely over HTTPS. All computation runs locally in your browser — your calculator inputs are never transmitted over a network. We implement industry-standard security headers including Content-Security-Policy, X-Content-Type-Options, Referrer-Policy, and Permissions-Policy.

While we take reasonable precautions, no method of data transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of any information.
